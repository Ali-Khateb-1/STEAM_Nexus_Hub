Project Title
AI-powered Digital Twin & Smart Prototyping Platform
Stage
Ideation & Early Development — Training models and preparing dynamic interfaces.
Description
A platform that helps users turn ideas into smart, sustainable product prototypes through reverse engineering, packaging design, brand building, and 3D file generation.
Target Users
Entrepreneurs, STEM educators, vocational schools, and digital fabrication labs.
Tech Stack
Python (TensorFlow, OpenCV, NumPy), JS, D3.js, cloud APIs, GitHub, 3D G-code tools.
Impact
Accelerates the path from idea to tangible solution, supports eco-conscious design, and bridges the digital-physical gap for innovators in Palestine and globally.
